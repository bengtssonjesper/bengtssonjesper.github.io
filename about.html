    About me

    My name is Jesper Bengtsson and I am a bird/wildlife-photographer. I live in Ã–rebro in Sweden where I am close to the mountainous ridge Kilsbergen. Here you can find a big selection of owls and other wildlife such as bears, lynx and wolves.

    Birds

    I love photographing birds and especially owls. Since I live so close to Kilsbergen I can find owls quite frequently and with time you learn their moving patterns which often times results in good pictures.

    Wildlife

    Being out in the wild and finding animals such as lynx, bears, wolves and others that you normally dont see if you dont spend time in the nature is something I find very intriguing and exciting. Since I usually spend a lot of time in front of the computer programming, this is a very nice way of doing something else for a while.
